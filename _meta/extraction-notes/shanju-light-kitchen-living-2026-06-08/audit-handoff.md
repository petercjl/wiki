## Audit focus

1. Verify that Shanju is not narrowed back to indoor kitchen only.
2. Verify that outdoor use remains “light outdoor cooking”, not rugged camping or heavy barbecue.
3. Verify that the style remains a kitchen-tool brand system, not a pet brand or baking-only brand.
4. Verify that AI prompts preserve product primacy while maintaining brand memory assets.
