## Known Gaps

- OCR did not recover on-screen prompt text. The formal page therefore describes the prompt role and workflow, but does not claim to preserve every displayed prompt string.
- ASR may contain small recognition errors. The formal page normalizes repeated terms using the structured chapter list and visible source context.
