# Micro Segment Plan｜财税股权 28 期课程知识系统

## Segmentation Rule

This recompile treats the four raw transcripts as one course source bundle but preserves source-file and line anchors. A micro-segment is a durable knowledge unit: concept, causal chain, case, number, tax treatment, equity rule, operating procedure, risk boundary, Q&A insight, or business diagnostic criterion.

Classroom logistics, sales pitch, applause, lunch, photo, small-red-flower exchange, and unrecoverable ASR noise are marked `raw-only` or `omitted-with-reason` in the coverage matrix.
