## Why Not Preserve D1/D2 Order

The classroom order interleaves finance, tax, equity, strategy, Q&A, cases, and sales/service sections. Preserving the chronological order would make future Agent retrieval harder. The new structure keeps line anchors in `coverage-matrix.md` while routing knowledge to topical pages.
