# 杉居轻厨房生活风生图 Playbook

本页用于生成杉居品牌风格的厨房工具、烧烤盘、调味罐、烘焙工具、收纳和餐厨小物图片。

## Prompt 骨架

```text
为杉居品牌生成一张【图位】。产品是【产品】。
风格：杉居轻厨房生活风，继承极简北欧风的哑光白、浅木、自然光、大留白、低饱和和清爽秩序。
品牌固定项：明亮白木厨房或露台/庭院轻料理场景，浅蓝宽松衬衫年轻女性，橘猫陪伴，鼠尾草绿小物，白陶瓷/玻璃杯/浅木托盘，短句式轻生活语气。
场景：【室内厨房轻烘焙 / 露台轻烧烤 / 庭院野餐 / 调味罐户外料理 / 厨房收纳】。
情绪：温柔、干净、松弛、安心托付、周末分享。
画面要求：产品是明确主角，但自然嵌入料理动作；光线柔和，色彩低饱和，人物动作小而真实，猫是陪伴角色，不抢产品。
负向约束：不要粗犷露营风，不要夜市烧烤摊，不要重烟雾油脂，不要高饱和色，不要黑金奢华，不要促销大字，不要网红摆拍。
```

## 室内厨房图

```text
Create a realistic Shanju brand lifestyle product image in a bright minimal Scandinavian kitchen. The product is [product]. Use matte white counters, pale wood shelves, soft window light, a young woman in a loose pale blue shirt, a calm orange cat nearby, sage green kitchen accents, white ceramic cups, glassware, fresh flowers, and light baking food. The product should be the hero while naturally participating in a gentle cooking or baking moment. Clean, warm, low-saturation, large negative space, live gently mood. No clutter, no hard advertising text, no luxury black and gold, no heavy smoke.
```

## 露台/庭院轻烧烤图

```text
Create a realistic Shanju brand outdoor light cooking scene on a bright terrace or small garden table. The product is [grill pan / seasoning jar / kitchen tool]. Keep the minimal Scandinavian feeling: pale wood table, off-white linen, sage green accents, white ceramic plates, soft afternoon light, light grilled vegetables or bread, a young woman in a loose pale blue shirt, and an orange cat near the doorway or garden edge. The mood is clean, gentle, organized, and warm, not rugged camping. Product as hero, low prop density, no heavy smoke, no black outdoor gear, no greasy barbecue stall mood.
```

## 调味罐户外料理图

```text
Generate a Shanju brand image for seasoning jars used from kitchen to outdoor dining. Scene: a pale wood picnic or terrace table, white ceramic plates, light grilled vegetables, bread slices, glass cup, sage green cloth, soft daylight, minimal Scandinavian composition. Show the seasoning jars neatly arranged and being used by a young woman in a pale blue shirt. Add a calm orange cat as a distant companion if natural. Mood: bring favorite flavors to the weekend breeze, clean and gentle outdoor cooking, no clutter, no rustic camping, no smoky barbecue.
```

## QA 清单

合格图应满足：

- 产品清楚，且与料理动作有关系。
- 空间可在室内厨房、露台、庭院或野餐桌，但都必须保持清爽和北欧式低饱和。
- 浅蓝衬衫女性、橘猫、白木空间、鼠尾草绿小物至少出现两个品牌记忆资产。
- 户外图不能变成硬核露营、夜市烧烤、重烟雾、黑色装备风。
- 文案如果出现，只能是短句式轻声表达；不要促销大字和参数堆叠。
